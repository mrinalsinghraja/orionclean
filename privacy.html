01

Overview

OrionClean ("the App") is developed and maintained by Mrinal Singh Raja ("we", "us", "our"). This Privacy Policy explains how OrionClean handles your information when you use the App on your Mac.

The short version: OrionClean collects absolutely nothing about you. All processing happens on your device. No data is ever transmitted to us or any third party.

02

Information We Do Not Collect

OrionClean does not collect, store, transmit, or share any of the following:

  • Personal identification information — name, email address, phone number, or any other identifiers
  • Usage data or analytics — how you use the app, which features you tap, session duration, or crash reports sent externally
  • File contents — OrionClean scans file metadata (names, sizes, dates) to identify junk files but never reads or transmits the contents of your files
  • System information — hardware specs, serial numbers, or device identifiers are never transmitted
  • Location data — location permission (if granted) is used solely to identify your WiFi network name and is never stored or transmitted
  • Advertising identifiers — no IDFA, fingerprinting, or advertising tracking of any kind
  • Payment information — the App is free and processes no payments
03

Local Data Storage

OrionClean stores certain data locally on your device only using Apple's UserDefaults system. This data never leaves your Mac and includes:

  • App settings and preferences — your scan configuration, safety settings, and UI preferences
  • Scan history — a log of previous scans and cleanup results for your own reference
  • Achievement progress — gamification state and engagement metrics
  • Weekly report data — aggregated summaries of your cleanup activity

You can clear all locally stored data at any time by uninstalling OrionClean or resetting app data in macOS System Settings.

04

On-Device AI

OrionClean uses Apple's FoundationModels framework (Apple Intelligence) to power its AI features, including the AI Assistant, file safety analysis, system diagnostics, and weekly reports.

All AI processing runs entirely on your Mac. No prompts, file information, or results are sent to any external server — including Apple's servers or ours. The AI has no internet connection.

On devices or macOS versions where FoundationModels is unavailable, OrionClean falls back to local heuristic analysis. No network requests are made in either case.

05

Network Activity

OrionClean makes no requests to external servers for data collection, analytics, or tracking. The only network-related activity in the App is:

  • Network Quality testing — uses outbound client connections to measure your internet connection performance (bandwidth, latency, packet loss). This is a diagnostic-only feature. No personal data is transmitted — only standard network probe packets.
  • WiFi name detection — reads your current WiFi network name locally for display purposes only. Never stored or transmitted.

The Network Quality feature does not act as a VPN, packet sniffer, firewall, or server. It does not listen for incoming connections. There are no analytics SDKs, crash reporting services, ad networks, or third-party frameworks in OrionClean that communicate with external servers.

06

File System Access

OrionClean uses App Sandbox and requests access to your file system only through standard macOS user-granted folder access. Specifically:

  • User-selected folder access — when a scan requires access to a folder outside the app container, OrionClean prompts you via the standard macOS open panel. You choose which folders to grant access to. This permission is persisted and reused through a security-scoped access flow.
  • App container — OrionClean can always access its own app container for settings, history, and cached data.
  • No Full Disk Access required — OrionClean does not request or require Full Disk Access. All scanning and cleanup operates within user-granted folder permissions only.

File access is used exclusively for local scanning and cleanup. No file contents, names, or paths are transmitted externally. You are always in control — OrionClean never automatically deletes anything without your confirmation.

07

Third-Party Services

OrionClean does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, crash reporting, or data collection services. There are no third-party SDKs in the App that collect or transmit user data.

The App is distributed through the Mac App Store. Apple's own privacy practices govern the App Store experience and any data Apple collects as part of distribution. Please refer to Apple's Privacy Policy at apple.com/privacy for details.

08

Children's Privacy

OrionClean does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13. The App collects no personal information from any user regardless of age. The App is rated 4+ on the Mac App Store.
